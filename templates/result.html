💡 How the Adaptive Logic Works

  • First, the system extracts skills from the resume and job description.
  • Then it identifies missing job-relevant skills.
  • Next, it automatically adds prerequisite skills that the learner may also need.
  • It generates a dependency-aware ordered roadmap so foundational skills come before advanced ones.
  • Finally, it estimates time, computes metrics, and simulates mastery-based adaptive progression.
