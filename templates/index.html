💡 How PathForge Works

  • Claude AI semantically extracts skills from resume and job description — not just keyword matching
  • Identifies the gap between your current skills and what the role requires
  • Prerequisite expansion: if "machine learning" is missing, Python, NumPy & Pandas are automatically added too
  • Topological DFS sort ensures foundational skills always come before advanced ones
  • TF-IDF cosine similarity scores how closely each course matches the specific job
  • Mastery simulation provides adaptive feedback — know when to push ahead vs revise
